Profile
Introduction

I'm Charles Luo, an international student hailing from China and currently navigating the exciting world of creativity at the University of Toronto. Beyond the textbooks, I'm a true enthusiast for basketball and immersive video games, drawing inspiration from legends like Steph Curry. I believe in using my passion and actions to positively influence those around me.My academic focus converges on the digital design arena. Armed with solid expertise in UX design, Visual Design, Web Design, and Prototyping Design, I complement these skills with a deep understanding of visual art and cinema studies.

Study
Description

My exploration into media production goes beyond the ordinary. From web design to video editing and post-production, I've delved into various facets of the digital landscape. Proficient in Final Cut Pro and After Effects for video editing, and adept at using Photoshop for photo editing and color grading. In the realm of web design, I've used Adobe XD for page layout and prototyping, coupled with Dreamweaver for front-end web design. Venturing into game design, I've mastered the initial functions of Unity and seamlessly integrated Maya modeling to create simple immersive virtual spaces.
